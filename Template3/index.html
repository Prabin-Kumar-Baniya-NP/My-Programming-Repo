Burger New 4.7

Burger is short for a hamburger. It's a fast food sandwich. That is a ground beef patty, grilled, between a split seeded hamburger bun, often optionally also layered with lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and a variety of sauces such as mayonnaise, mustard, or ketchup. The burger itself is made of ground meat.
